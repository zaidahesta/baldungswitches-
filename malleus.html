Malleus Maleficarum: The Hammer of Witches

The Malleus Maleficarum is a late 15th century manuscript by Heinrich Kramer and Jacobus Sprenger.
This treatise by the Dominican priests is the standard late medieval text on witchcraft,
and it remained in print during the early modern period. The manuscript describes the evil nature and acts
of witches in great detail, focusing on the wicked nature of women and their sexuality.
As well as theological explanations on the nature of witchcraft, evil, and the devil,
the Malleus also contains a thorough guide on how to find, torture and execute witches.
